Personal

Unity (C#)

Jan 2015

2 developers, 1 artist, 1 game designer
Car workshop simulator game. Each month is a stage where customers arrived with requests to satisfy in terms of power, safety, fuel consumption.

The goal is to be as accurate as possible in satisfying the request by trying to spend as little as possible. With the money obtained it is possible to unlock new components for increasingly demanding or extreme requests.
