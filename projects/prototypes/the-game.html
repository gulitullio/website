The digital transposition of the card game.

It is a game in which there are four piles of cards, two with increasing numbers from 1 to 99 and two with decreasing numbers from 100 to 2.

The participants try to play as many cards as possible following some simple rules, in some piles they can play only increasing cards in other only descending.

The target is obstruct the opponent and not make him place more cards.
