StarBugs

Unity (C#)

Sep 2022

High Stone Games Team: 2 developers, 2 artists, 1 designer
A prototype of a NFT card game. The theme of the game are space insects that will have to challenge the champions of different worlds and arenas.

Each champion is characterized by attributes such as level, life, attack, energy cost, family and class.

Each team will have a core to protect, you will win by defeating all the enemies or by exhausting the energy of the opponent's core.
