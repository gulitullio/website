• Don't Die! Zombie Survival

    Unity (C#)

    Dec 2022

    It is a survival simulation game where will be necessary to gather resources, hunt, fight, and build shelters, weapons or traps. It will be also important to worry about hunger, thirst, any injuries, cold or atmospheric conditions.

    The game has been released on patreon with the intent to continue development with more maps to visit, a storyline, new mechanics, new weapons and new items.
