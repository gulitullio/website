Bingo Town

Unity (C#), GameSparks

Nov 2020

WhatWapp Team: 1 producer, 3 developers, 2 artists
A multiplayer Bingo game with very high quality graphics.

The features are: level progression, towns, daily missions, puzzles, boosters, minigames such as the treasure map and the creation of smoothies and relative fruit market
