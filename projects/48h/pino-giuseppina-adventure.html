Pino e Giuseppina Adventure

Microsoft XNA (.NET)

Aug 2011

Team: 4 developers, 4 graphic designers, 2 illustrators
First partecipation with a group of passionate people: the Psycho game studios.

It is a side-scrolling platform game for 2 players playing in the same keyboard.

The goal is to complete different levels where you have to face enemies and solve puzzles to discover the hidden treasure
