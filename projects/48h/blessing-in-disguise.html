Blessing in disguise

Personal

Unreal Engine (C++)

Jul 2017

1 developer, 1 game designer
An idea developed during the Summer UE4jam with the theme "Blessing in disguise", first official use of Unreal Engine.

The idea was a game where you had to save pedestrians from objects thrown from the windows. The player could intervene for example by throwing banana peels and saving the victim.
