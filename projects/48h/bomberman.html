Bomberman

Wakefullness

Unity (C#)

Dec 2013

3 developers, 3 3D modelers, 4 artists
Made with the amateur team Wakefullness in its first internal edition of the 48h.

It is a 3D Bomberman reinterpreted with new mechanics.

Both players compete on the same keyboard on 5 possible scenarios and they can earn points by finding hidden resources behind the destructible blocks and take points away from the opponent by killing him. In addition, it is possible to find upgrades to place more bombs or increase their range.
