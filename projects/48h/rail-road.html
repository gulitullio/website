Rail Road

Unity (C#)

Mar 2014

Team: 3 developers, 3 3D modelers, 2 artists
Race simulator made with the Wakefullness amateur team for the second internal edition of the 48h.

Everyone in their childhood has played and loved slot cars. The game replicate the bedroom of a child intent on building his own track of slot cars.

The game consists in two main sections:

- a track editor where you can attach the pieces composed by straight, tight and wide curves
- a race phase where it is only possible to throttle up and down to the finish
