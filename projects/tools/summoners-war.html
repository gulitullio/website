Personal

Unity (C#)

Jul 2022

1 developer
A tool that I made to evaluate easily and faster the runes of the Summoner Wars game.

The rune system is very complex because it includes different types of runes and variable upgrade rules depending on the position of the rune, the type and the rarity.

The system makes a prediction of how the rune will evolve in the worst and best case and it gives a score to the rune
